THE INQUIRY

We are a private investigations group that operates primarily online. We work for free. We only investigate pedophiles. All our findings are reported to Cybertip and the local police of the perpetrator. Whether you know someone who was a victim of CSA or are one, you probably have a close connection to the horrifying people we hunt.

If you have encountered child pornography, please report it to report.cybertip.org immediately. Sending us any such illegal material is considered distribution and is a federal crime according to 18 U.S.C. § 2252.

To provide tips to our team, email evidence of someone's pedophilia to theinquiry@protonmail.com. Once again, we must stress that sending any illegal content is a federal crime. Once we recieve your tip, we will look through the evidence and investigate the person you have accused. If sufficient evidence of a crime has been collected, as well as a bit of their information, we report them to Cybertip and their local police. In the case of international perpetrators, we will contact the proper authorities including FBI international offices. Tipsters will stay anonymous.

Please stay safe out there. It's a dangerous world, but your tips make it a safer place for all children.
